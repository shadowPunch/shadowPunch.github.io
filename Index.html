Profile

Physics first. Systems that ship, second nature.

I'm a third-year Engineering Physics student at IIT Roorkee, which means I arrived at AI through control theory and signal processing rather than the other way round. That shows up in how I build: optical flow running on an FPGA's DPU, a voice agent that has to answer in under a second, a document pipeline that has to run on a laptop CPU.

The constraint is usually the interesting part. I work across the stack — model, pipeline, deployment — and I'd rather ship something at 70% accuracy on real hardware than 99% in a notebook.
